Production ERP + Forecasting

Star N Hero

Activewear & apparel retail — built the production, inventory, and demand system from scratch
17Linked sheets
129SKUs tracked

What it does

A full production-to-inventory loop for a fashion brand selling across Shopee, TikTok, and live events. Every finished-goods SKU is backed by a Bill of Materials; every production run checks real material availability before it's allowed to start.

  • BOM-driven material engine — each SKU resolves its raw-material consumption automatically; running a production plan recalculates stock, reservations, and a live AMAN / STOK RENDAH / KURANG status before anything is approved.
  • Guarded production lifecycle — DRAFT → DALAM PROSES → SELESAI → POSTED, enforced through onEdit() triggers so status can't be hand-edited once material is reserved or stock has been posted.
  • Custom sidebar app — an HTML/JS sidebar (talking to Apps Script via google.script.run) for creating, editing, and copying BOMs without ever touching the raw data sheet.
  • Forecasting & MRP layer — a separate workbook (17 sheets) computes monthly demand rate per SKU, recommends production quantities, and projects inventory health (dead stock, overstock, capital locked) feeding a live Looker Studio dashboard.

Production Workflow Tracker

Soulmate Bedsheet

Bedsheet manufacturer — order-to-delivery tracking across five production divisions
5Stages tracked
AutoOrder ID + pricing
2Linked spreadsheets
What it does

Every order moves through Potong → Jahit → Packing → Kirim → (Konversi) — and each division only ever sees its own queue, while the master Orders sheet stays the single source of truth.

  • Auto-generated order IDs with a Roman-numeral month code (e.g. SOULMATE/VI/26/014) issued the moment a customer and product are entered — no manual numbering, no duplicates.
  • Division-scoped views — Potong, Jahit, Packing, Kirim, and Konversi each get a filtered, auto-refreshed sheet; entering a PIC there writes the timestamp back to Orders and advances the step, with stock-availability branching the flow straight to delivery when no production is needed.
  • Cross-sheet reconciliation — a scheduled job (buildDivisionData) merges Orders with an external "Master Data" spreadsheet, deduplicates by Order ID, and feeds a Looker Studio–ready table with per-division value attribution.
  • Self-correcting edits — clearing a PIC cascades forward, wiping every downstream timestamp and PIC so the step always reflects reality, not a stale checkbox.

Finance & Invoicing System

Vindes Corp — Finance Division

Talent & media agency — project intake to invoice to payment, with PPN tax logic built in
2Business units
4Custom sidebars
11%PPN auto-calculated
What it does

A relational system across six linked sheets that takes a deal from client intake through approval, invoicing, and payment reconciliation — for two business units (Vitamin, Vakansi) with different document and folder rules.

  • Client deduplication engine — normalizes company names (strips "PT", punctuation, casing) to detect existing clients and auto-assign a recurring "Anchor" ID instead of creating duplicates.
  • Tax-aware invoice math — handles three deal structures (NON GROSS UP, GROSS UP, ALL IN) and derives DPP, PPN, and Grand Total correctly for each, including official invoice numbering in Indonesian fiscal format (VTMND/VI/2026/003).
  • Approval-gated workflow — a project can't be invoiced until it's Approved; an invoice can't be paid past its outstanding balance without an explicit override prompt — both enforced in the sidebar logic, not just by convention.
  • Payment term rebuilding — cancelling or adding a payment automatically rebuilds the invoice's Term 1–5 amount/date slots from the live Payment Tracker, so the invoice sheet never drifts from the ledger.
  • Drive + Gmail integration — uploaded NPWP/SPK/CL/quotation files are routed to business-unit-specific Drive folders and renamed by convention; approvals, rejections, and invoice releases trigger templated emails automatically.
